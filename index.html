YEMEN CRISIS: ENDING STARVATION

What is Going On in Yemen?

Yemen is continuing it's Civil war which has been going on since 2011. However, because of the pandemic of COVID-19, food supplies have dropped, clean water is becoming harder to find, and the death toll rising to extreme numbers on the already high number of war casualties.

Image taken from this website.

How is it Effecting Yemen People?

Malnutrition, poor health, and air strikes from the war are the leading causes of these deaths. Air strikes have caused many homes to be destroyed, leaving many families homeless, hungry, and near the brink of death. Yemen families consiting of many children have been the most affected of this crisis, many children dying in the arms of their mother's. About 80% of the population of Yemen is in need of need humanitarian assistance.
